Our History

Today we are an established, industry leading full-service machine shop. Though it has not always been this way. Joe Porterfield founded Solutions Machining over 30 years ago in his residential garage. Joe's garage was the corner stone for our growing success today. When Joe Porterfield founded the company in Arvada, Colorado, he bought two small manual machines. The machines he bought were, a single table Bridgeport Mill and the other was a small six-inch chuck, extended bed, LeBlond Lathe. We still have the Bridgeport Mill in the back of the shop as a memorial to Joe's hard-work and dedication that Solutions Machine was founded upon. (Yes, it is still fully-functional!) These values of hard-work and honesty live on in the shop and are still at the core of our team today. Ownership of Solutions Machining has always been passed down to former employees. Today Dale Williamson, a former employee with fifteen years of service, is now at the lead of our team as owner/manager. Dale has invested in and grown Solutions Machine considerably as we now service our customers with over ten CNC programmable machining centers from industry leading suppliers, such as Mori-Seiki and Mazak. We are still based out of Arvada, Colorado and we continue to serve multiple original customers. Customers that Joe once had the pleasure servicing. Visit our "Contact Us" page to schedule an in person tour of the shop and meet our team today!

Our Community

Dale Williamson: Owner/Manager

Hello and Thank You for your interest in our Team. My name is Dale Williamson, Owner/Manager of Solutions Machine, Inc. I'm a Colorado native and possess a deep appreciation for this state. In my younger years I was an avid outdoors man of many activities, ranging from guiding raft trips to extended through hiking. I have always had an entrepreneur drive as this is my third attempt at business ownership. My past two attempts had not succeeded. Though, I was not willing to give up. A good entrepreneur is not discouraged by failure but learns from it.

I have successfully owned and managed Solutions Machining for over fifteen years now. I'm still gracious for the opportunity to do so. It is my promise to my customers and employees that we will continue to be at the cutting edge of industry standards, to meet and exceed their needs. My employees are what make the difference here by comparison to other shops and I value each of them greatly. They are the driving force behind our excellent customer satisfaction.
